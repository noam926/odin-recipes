Oats Porridge

Description

This very simple recipe uses only 2-3 materials in order to make a nice and nutritious breakfast

Ingredients

  • Quacker Oats
  • Water
  • (Optional) Banana

Steps

  1. Pour the oats into a bowl - 80g
  2. Pour lukewarm water into the bowl, stop when it starts to flood the bowl - mix the components
  3. Put the bowl in the microwave for 1:30 minutes
  4. (Optional) Slice a banana into the bowl
  5. Enjoy!
