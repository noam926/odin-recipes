Shakshuka

Description

This dish is a known breakfast in the medditerranean, consists of several components which are very simple to use. An excellent breakfast full of protein and vitamins, as well as spicy!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes/Cherru Tomatoes - we want to have a lot so we can get all the liquid out of them
  • 2 to 5 eggs
  • Spicy Paprika
  • Sweet Paprika
  • Parsley
  • Hot Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Light the medium stove top on medium heat
  2. Put all the tomatoes in a wide pan (if those tomatoes are big, slice them a to chunks) - let the liquid come out
  3. Pour some hot water, to reduce the thicknnes of the sauce
  4. Crack the eggs and spill them into the pan
  5. Add a liitle salt on top of the spilled eggs
  6. Add both Paprikas as much as you want
  7. Close the pan, let it cook a few minutes
  8. After it's cooked, add the clisced parsley on top of everything
  9. Enjoy!
